Peanut Butter Cookies

Delicious cookies easy to make!

Ingredients and cooking instructions

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar

Cooking Instructions

  1. mix sugars, butter and peanut butter until combined
  2. beat in egg and add in vanilla extract
  3. add in chocolate chips and reeces pieces
  4. form into balls and place on baking sheet
  5. cook at 175 for 12 mins
  6. rest and enjoy!
